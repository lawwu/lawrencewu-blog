the extension most engineering talks miss

It's not just engineers

  • AI-native companies encode sales, support, ops, and finance as skills — written procedures their agents execute.
  • They hire engineers whose job is to maintain those skills — and do the work skills can't do yet.
  • At YC: media staff, event staff, finance — people who've never opened a terminal — now write skill files and cron jobs.
  • One finance staffer collapsed ~100 Excel workbooks into a single app. "She's not a programmer. She's a manager of agents now — and everyone at YC is."

where almost every AI engineering bug comes from

Two places computation happens

LATENT SPACE

The LLM

Taste. Judgment. Understanding what a human means when they say something vague. Non-deterministic — steered with the markdown file.

DETERMINISTIC SPACE

The code

Where agents write TypeScript, or Elixir, or whatever. Exact math and logic — it must not live in the context window.

Nearly every bug is computation happening on the wrong side of this line.

example

Seating 800 people perfectly — in ten minutes

Startup School: seat 800 people so the person to your left and right is the best possible person for you to meet.

  • The combinatorics — an 800-seat multi-dimensional array — runs in deterministic space. It must not live in the context window.
  • The matching judgment — who should sit next to whom — is latent space.
  • By hand, this used to take a month. Now: a couple hundred dollars of tokens and about ten minutes.

"this is just RAG" — yes, and

A company brain is the library plus the librarian

Retrieval is the primitive — the same way Postgres is just B-trees. The hard part is everything around it:

  • What gets written down in the first place, into the knowledge wiki
  • How it gets enriched and linked
  • What's promoted to hot memory vs. filed as cold reference
  • Who arbitrates when two facts disagree

Retrieval is easy. Being worth retrieving from is the product.

stress-testing his own pitch

Company brains fail in specific ways

WITHOUT HYGIENE

A garbage dump with great search

Retrieval surfaces stale facts with total confidence. A bad skill file encodes a bad process forever.

THE FIX: MEMORY + HYGIENE

Provenance and pruning

Provenance on every fact. Contradiction checks when new info collides with old. A librarian — human plus agent — whose job is pruning.

Treat the brain like production infrastructure and it compounds. Treat it like a dumping ground and you get a confident agent that's wrong in ways nobody can trace.
